# The Crypt of Fate - COYA Game

Choose Your Own Adventure interactive story made with pure HTML. No CSS, no JavaScript.

---

## About The Game

The Crypt of Fate is an interactive narrative game inspired by the classic "Choose Your Own Adventure" books from the 80s and 90s, and modern works like Black Mirror: Bandersnatch.

You play as an archaeologist who discovers an ancient crypt hidden in the jungle. Every decision changes your destiny. 

---

## How to Play

1. Start at **index.html**
2. Read the story and look at the images
3. Click on links (a tags) to make decisions
4. Reach the ending
5. Play again and discover all paths
